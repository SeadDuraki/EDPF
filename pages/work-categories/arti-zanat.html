A visual and narrative journey that brings into focus the cultural heritage of Kosovo and the rare crafts facing the threat of extinction. In a world that is constantly changing, this exhibition sheds light on the artisans who work with dedication and passion to preserve their precious traditions and heritage.

The high-contrast black-and-white photographs serve as a powerful medium, allowing us to see beyond the colors of everyday life, emphasizing the texture and intricate details of traditional crafts. In the context of this exhibition, they symbolize a deep connection between the past and the present, highlighting the timeless value and sense of history embodied in each craft. Each image is a window into the daily lives of the artisans, where their centuries-old traditions collide with the fast pace of modern life.
